This game will primarily use the _2024 D&D 5e ruleset_. However, a number of house rules will be in place to support immersion, apply common sense, and better reflect the unique tone of the setting.

Some adjustments have also been made to accommodate the _Eberron_ campaign setting, which draws heavily from 2014 (and earlier) source material. Most notably, _half-elves_ and _half-orcs_, integral to Eberron’s lore, will remain available as player options despite their removal from the core 2024 rules.

These rules are flexible and open to discussion. The goal is to enhance everyone’s enjoyment of the game, and adjustments may be made as the campaign evolves.
