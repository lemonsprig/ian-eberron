title: Key Levels
---
I am not a massive fan of attunement slots in 5e, and in all honesty wish I could do away with them and go back to the good old days where you could pretty much wear one item per body location. Unfortunately one of the roadblocks here is D&D beyond, and short of going through and making custom items for every item so that they do not need to be attuned there is not a lot that can be done, and besides it does massively screw with balance.

To offset this we will be using a rule where at key levels 5, 10 and 15 you will be able to add a feat for free representing your experiences as you become more seasoned adventurers. I am still trying to work out how to homebrew this (again D&D beyond is a a bit quirky) but essentially at each of these levels the following will occur.

- You will gain an additional attunement slot
  
- You will be able to choose between
	- Gaining a new proficiency
	- Gaining a tool proficiency and a language
