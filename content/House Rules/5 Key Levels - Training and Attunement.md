I am not a massive fan of attunement slots in 5e, and in all honesty wish I could do away with them and go back to the good old days where you could pretty much wear one item per body location. Unfortunately one of the roadblocks here is D&D beyond, and short of going through and making custom items for every item so that they do not need to be attuned there is not a lot that can be done, and besides it does massively screw with balance.

Similarly it feels a bit flat that once your initial skills are taken you don't really get to add new skills, tools or have the warrior teach you how to use a new weapon through training.

To offset this as you adventure and become more seasoned adventurers you will gain for free the following feats.

## Training (levels 5, 10, 15 and 20)
Choose from the following:

- Become proficient in one additional skill
- Become proficient in a weapon
- Become proficient in two tools
- Become proficient in two languages
- Become proficient in one tool and one language

## Additional Attunement (levels 10 and 20)

- You gain an additional attunement slot
