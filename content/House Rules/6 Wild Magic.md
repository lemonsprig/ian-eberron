---
title: Wild Magic
---
The sorcerer wild magic surge table has been expanded so as to provide a unique list of 100 options. The target of these effects are also randomised in all bar a few instances where the effect applies to the caster only. Finally a d6 is rolled to determines if an oddity (cosmetic effect) occurs as part of the surge.

A surge is triggered using the normal rules as described under the wild magic sorcerer. When one occurs, perform the following

1. Roll a D100 and consult the Surge Table below.
2. If the table indicates the surge applies to a target role a D8 and determine the target as follows

| d8  | Who is affected?                 | Control of summoned creatures / centred effects                    |
| --- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | **Self** (the caster)            | Summon fights for the caster.                                      |
| 2   | **Caster’s Choice**              | The caster chooses the creature or point; summon obeys the caster. |
| 3   | **Nearest Enemy**                | Enemy closest to the caster; summon fights for the caster.         |
| 4   | **Random Enemy**                 | Random enemy in sight; summon fights for the caster.               |
| 5   | **Nearest Ally (not self)**      | Summon fights for that ally.                                       |
| 6   | **Random Ally (not self)**       | Summon fights for that ally.                                       |
| 7   | **Nearest Creature (any side)**  | Summon obeys that creature.                                        |
| 8   | **Furthest Creature (any side)** | Summon obeys that creature.                                        |
3 . Roll a D6 to check for an oddity, On a roll of 1 an oddity affects the caster, on a roll of 6 an oddity affects the target. All other numbers result in no oddity occurring. If an oddity occurs consult the oddity table below
