---
title: Bastions
---
The Bastion rules as introduced in the 2024 DMG are not being used. However during the course of play players may receive or gain access to similar rewards. Details of this and how they may be enhanced, costs of doing such and benefits will be fleshed out as and when needed, the idea is to make this something the characters are invested in as a group or individually, for instance 

- A group owned Keep
- A tavern 
- An abandoned laboratory or forge of creation
- A privately owned airship or lightning rail carriage

I feel this offers more scope than simply gaining land rooms and some powerful money making/item creation features (ahem a greater healing potion every 7 days) just simply because you have hit a certain level.

It also means that there is a lot more investment in these features, particularly when they are at risk of being damaged, destroyed as opposed to bastion rules where you suffer a minor setback such as not being able to use a feature for 7 days or x random guards die but are replaced the next time you do a recruit action. 

I think it far more exciting when you for instance take ownership of an abandoned tower and then convince a group of gargoyles to become employees as tower guards, or build a nesting roost and start training and equipping griffin riders that you can call on when you need to quickly travel somewhere or need arial defence for a nearby town. It becomes even more fun when those griffins get struck down with a mysterious bird flu, or a rogue wizard from the university decides he wants to experiment on gargoyles and stone to flesh magic, and your guards become his playthings.... 

Now we are talking about problems you are invested in solving.
