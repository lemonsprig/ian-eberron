# House Allegiances

_A guide to joining and working with the Dragonmarked Houses._

## Understanding House Structure

The Dragonmarked Houses function as multinational corporations, noble families, and magical institutions simultaneously. They operate across all national borders, making them some of the most powerful organizations in post-war Khorvaire.

### House Hierarchy

**House Leadership:**

- **Baron/Baroness:** Head of the entire House
- **Viceroys:** Regional leaders governing House operations in specific areas
- **Patriarchs/Matriarchs:** Leaders of major family branches

**Membership Levels:**

- **Heirs:** Blood relatives who bear the dragonmark
- **Scions:** Blood relatives without the mark
- **Retainers:** Non-family members employed by the House
- **Associates:** Independent contractors and regular clients

## Major Houses and Their Services

### House Cannith - The Makers

**Dragonmark:** Mark of Making  
**Specialties:** Artifice, construction, warforged creation  
**Motto:** "We build the future"

**Joining Cannith:**

- **Requirements:** Exceptional skill in artifice, magic item creation, or construction
- **Entry Path:** Apprenticeship program, university recruitment, or demonstrable innovation
- **Benefits:** Access to creation forges, magical research facilities, and exclusive schematics
- **Obligations:** Innovation quotas, trade secret protection, House loyalty over national ties

**Services to Members:**

- Advanced magical item creation
- Warforged repair and modification
- Access to rare materials and components
- Patent protection and licensing deals

### House Deneith - The Sentinel

## Non-Marked House Membership

### Becoming a Retainer

**Requirements:**

- Useful skills complementing House operations
- Demonstrated loyalty and reliability
- Willingness to put House interests first

**Benefits:**

- Regular employment and income
- House protection and legal representation
- Access to House facilities and services
- Professional development opportunities

**Obligations:**

- Follow House directives without question
- Maintain House secrets and confidentiality
- Report threats to House interests
- Represent the House professionally

### Associate Status

**Requirements:**

- Regular business with the House
- Good payment history
- Professional reputation

**Benefits:**

- Preferential service rates
- Priority booking and scheduling
- Access to exclusive services
- House connections and networking

**Obligations:**

- Maintain professional relationship
- Pay bills promptly
- Respect House policies
- Refer quality clients

## House Politics and Rivalries

### Major Rivalries

- **Phiarlan vs. Thuranni:** The Shadow Schism continues to influence both Houses
- **Cannith vs. Competition:** Multiple factions within Cannith compete for dominance
- **Lyrandar vs. Orien:** Transportation competition, especially over long distances

### Political Considerations

- Houses officially remain neutral in national politics
- Individual House members may have personal political beliefs
- House interests sometimes conflict with national interests
- Characters must balance House loyalty with other allegiances

- **Kundarak:** Investigate vault breaches, recover stolen assets, financial intelligence
- **Lyrandar:** Weather investigation, airship security, elemental binding research
- **Medani:** Private investigations, missing persons, intelligence gathering
- **Orien:** Route security, message delivery, transportation infrastructure
- **Phiarlan:** Entertainment bookings, information gathering, diplomatic missions
- **Sivis:** Document authentication, communication security, translation services

- **Thuranni:** Elimination contracts, shadow operations, artistic competitions

### House Conflicts

- Internal power struggles between family branches
- Competition between Houses for lucrative contracts

## Character Integration

### Choosing a House Allegiance

Consider how your character's background, skills, and goals align with House operations. Multiple House connections are possible but create potential conflicts of interest.

### Advancement Within Houses

- **Performance:** Complete missions successfully
- **Innovation:** Contribute new ideas or techniques
- **Loyalty:** Demonstrate consistent House support
- **Networking:** Build valuable relationships
- **Leadership:** Take initiative in House operations

### Leaving a House

House membership isn't permanent, but departure carries consequences:

- Loss of House benefits and protections
- Potential blacklisting from House services
- Possible retaliation if departing with secrets
- Difficulty joining rival Houses

_For detailed information about specific House operations and internal politics, see **Eberron: Rising from the Last War** and **Dragonmarked**_
