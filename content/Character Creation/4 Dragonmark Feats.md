# Dragonmark Feats

_Magical birthmarks that grant supernatural abilities_

## Understanding Dragonmark Feats

Dragonmark feats represent the manifestation of magical birthmarks that appear in specific bloodlines. These feats can only be taken by characters of the appropriate race and are typically gained through backgrounds, though they can be selected during character advancement with DM approval.

### Dragonmark Progression

- **Dragonmark:** Base-level abilities, available at character creation
- **Greater Dragonmark:** Enhanced abilities, available at higher levels with prerequisites
- **Siberys Dragonmark (Epic Boon):** Legendary abilities, available at 19th level

_Note: Only one dragonmark feat may be taken per character. Greater Dragonmarks require the corresponding base dragonmark as a prerequisite._

## Base Dragonmark Feats

### Dragonmark of Detection

_Prerequisite: Half-elf or Elf, House Medani heritage_

Your dragonmark grants you supernatural perception and investigative abilities.

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma increases by 1 (your choice).

**Spells:** You learn the _detect magic_ and _detect poison and disease_ spells. You can cast each of these spells once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast these spells using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

**Intuitive Detection:** When you make a Wisdom (Insight) or Wisdom (Perception) check, you can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the total. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

**Spellcasting Ability:** Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma (your choice when you gain this feat)

## Aberrant Dragonmark

### Aberrant Dragonmark

_Prerequisite: Any race_

You bear an aberrant dragonmark—a twisted and unpredictable manifestation of dragonmark power that marks you as an outcast.

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Constitution increases by 1.

**Spells:** Choose one cantrip from the sorcerer spell list. You also learn one 1st-level spell from the sorcerer spell list. You can cast this 1st-level spell once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast it using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

**Aberrant Power:** When you cast the 1st-level spell granted by this feat, you can choose to have it affect one additional target (if the spell targets one creature) or increase its area of effect by 50% (if it affects an area). If you do so, you take psychic damage equal to your level. This damage cannot be reduced or prevented in any way.

**Flawed Manifestation:** Your aberrant mark is unstable. When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, the DM can choose to have your mark flare with painful energy. When this happens, you take 1 psychic damage and creatures within 5 feet of you must make a Constitution saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier) or take 1d4 psychic damage.

**Spellcasting Ability:** Constitution

## Greater Dragonmark Feats

_Prerequisites: 4th level or higher, corresponding base Dragonmark feat_

Greater Dragonmarks represent the maturation and strengthening of your dragonmark's power. Each Greater Dragonmark follows a similar pattern of enhancement while adding unique abilities.

### Greater Dragonmark of Detection

_Prerequisite: Dragonmark of Detection feat_

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma increases by 1 (your choice).

**Enhanced Detection:** The d4 you can add to Wisdom (Insight) and Wisdom (Perception) checks becomes a d6.

**Spells:** You learn the _detect thoughts_ and _see invisibility_ spells. You can cast each of these spells once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast these spells using any spell slots you have.

**Piercing Sight:** You gain blindsight with a range of 10 feet. Within this range, you can see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible.

### Greater Dragonmark of Finding

_Prerequisite: Dragonmark of Finding feat_

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma increases by 1 (your choice).

**Enhanced Tracking:** The d4 you can add to Wisdom (Survival) and Wisdom (Perception) checks becomes a d6.

**Spells:** You learn the _locate creature_ and _clairvoyance_ spells. You can cast each of these spells once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast these spells using any spell slots you have.

**Unerring Tracker:** Once per long rest, you can cast _locate creature_ without expending a spell slot, and the spell doesn't require concentration when cast this way.

### Greater Dragonmark of Handling

_Prerequisite: Dragonmark of Handling feat_

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma increases by 1 (your choice).

**Enhanced Connection:** The d4 you can add to Wisdom (Animal Handling) and Charisma checks with beasts becomes a d6.

**Spells:** You learn the _conjure animals_ and _dominate beast_ spells. You can cast each of these spells once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast these spells using any spell slots you have.

**Beast Bond:** You can communicate telepathically with any beast within 60 feet of you, provided the creature can understand at least one language.

### Greater Dragonmark of Healing

_Prerequisite: Dragonmark of Healing feat_

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma increases by 1 (your choice).

**Enhanced Healing:** The d4 you can add to Wisdom (Medicine) checks becomes a d6. When you use a healer's kit to stabilize a dying creature, that creature regains hit points equal to 1 + your spellcasting ability modifier.

**Spells:** You learn the _mass cure wounds_ and _greater restoration_ spells. You can cast each of these spells once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast these spells using any spell slots you have.

**Healing Aura:** As an action, you can create a 30-foot radius aura of healing energy that lasts for 1 minute. Friendly creatures that start their turn in the aura regain 1d4 hit points. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.

### Greater Dragonmark of Hospitality

_Prerequisite: Dragonmark of Hospitality feat_

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma increases by 1 (your choice).

**Enhanced Hospitality:** The d4 you can add to Charisma (Persuasion) checks and cook's utensils checks becomes a d6.

**Spells:** You learn the _Leomund's tiny hut_ and _heroes' feast_ spells. You can cast each of these spells once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. You can also cast these spells using any spell slots you have.

**Chaotic Surge:** When you use your Aberrant Power feature, you can choose to make the effect even more unpredictable. If you do, the spell affects all valid targets within range (for targeted spells) or doubles the area of effect (for area spells), but you take psychic damage equal to twice your level, and all creatures within 10 feet of you must make the Constitution save against your Flawed Manifestation.

## Epic Boon: Boon of Siberys

_Prerequisite: 19th level, any Greater Dragonmark feat_

Your dragonmark has evolved to its ultimate form, marking you as one of the legendary Siberys heirs. This boon represents the pinnacle of dragonmark evolution and grants you near-mythical power.

**Legendary Mark:** Your dragonmark transforms into a Siberys dragonmark, visibly different from lesser marks with golden, crystalline patterns that seem to shift and move.

**Wish Manifestation:** You can cast the _wish_ spell once per short rest without expending a spell slot. When you cast _wish_ in this way, there is no chance of being unable to cast it again, but the spell must be related to your dragonmark's theme and nature.

**Apex Ability:** Choose one ability related to your dragonmark's nature. You gain a legendary version of this ability that can dramatically alter reality within your domain. Work with your DM to design this ability.

**Dragonmark Mastery:** All spells granted by your dragonmark feats can be cast without expending spell slots. You regain all uses when you finish a long rest.

## Using Dragonmark Feats

**Spellcasting Focus:** You can use a dragonmark focus (a special focus incorporated into your dragonmark) as a spellcasting focus for the spells granted by your dragonmark feat.

**Multiclassing:** Dragonmark spells count as spells known for any spellcasting class you have, but only for the purposes of spell slot usage.

**Feat Progression:** Only one dragonmark feat may be taken per character. Greater Dragonmarks require their corresponding base dragonmark as a prerequisite and represent a significant investment in your character's magical development.

**Roleplay Considerations:** Your dragonmark is a visible sign of your heritage and abilities. Consider how NPCs and other characters react to your mark, especially in different nations and social situations. Greater Dragonmarks are particularly noteworthy and may draw significant attention.

**House Politics:** Bearing a dragonmark connects you to the broader politics and power structures of your House, creating both opportunities and obligations. Greater dragonmarks often come with increased expectations and responsibilities within your House hierarchy.
