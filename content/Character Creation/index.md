# Character Creation in Eberron

Your character enters a world forever changed by the Last War—a century-long conflict that ended just two years ago. Whether they fought, survived as civilians, or grew up during wartime, the conflict has touched every life in Khorvaire.

## Culture Over Blood

The war shattered old boundaries and mixed communities like never before. Refugees, soldiers, and survivors learned that **culture matters more than race**. Your character's beliefs and loyalties come from where they were raised and who they lived among, not their ancestry.

A goblin raised in Sharn may speak only Common, while a half-elf might be fully immersed in Khoravar traditions. In Eberron, **your nation, background, and personal choices define you far more than your bloodline**.

## The War's Mark

Consider how the Last War shaped your character:

**Veterans** carry physical and emotional scars, but also bonds forged with unlikely allies. Former enemies might now be trusted friends, while old comrades could have become rivals.

**Civilians** endured rationing, raids, and constant loss. They learned to see past differences when survival depended on neighbors helping neighbors, regardless of race or origin.

**War Children** grew up with warforged neighbors and dragonmarked classmates. To them, this diversity isn't remarkable—it's simply normal life.

## Connections That Matter

Consider what ties your character to this world:

- **Family**: Who raised you? Your "family" might not share your blood
- **Loyalties**: Do you serve a nation, dragonmarked house, or personal cause?
- **Wartime Bonds**: What relationships did the conflict create or destroy?
- **Party Relationships**: How do you know your fellow adventurers? Did you serve together, survive the same tragedy, or share a common cause? In Eberron, allies matter—trust is hard-won and precious.

## A World of Opportunity

The peace is fragile and the old order is changing. Dragonmarked houses recruit from all backgrounds, new nations offer fresh starts, and ancient mysteries await discovery. Your character enters at a pivotal moment when bold individuals can shape the future.

## Remember

- Background often matters more than class
- Every race can be found in every nation
- Magic and warforged are part of everyday life
- The war created shared experiences across old divides

Your character carries the potential to heal old wounds or forge new paths. The choice is yours.
