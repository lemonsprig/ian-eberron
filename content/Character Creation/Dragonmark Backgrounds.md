## Understanding Dragonmarks

Dragonmarks are magical birthmarks that manifest in specific bloodlines, granting supernatural abilities and serving as the foundation of the Dragonmarked Houses' power. Not everyone born to a dragonmarked family develops a mark—they appear in roughly 50% of eligible individuals, usually manifesting during adolescence or times of stress.

### Mark Manifestation

- **Dragonmark:** Base-level abilities, typically manifesting during character creation
- **Greater Dragonmark:** Enhanced abilities, available at higher levels with prerequisites
- **Siberys Dragonmark (Epic Boon):** Legendary abilities, available at 19th level

_Note: The old terminology of "Least," "Lesser," and "Greater" marks has been simplified to align with modern magical theory._

## Non-Marked Backgrounds

### Dragonmarked Scion

**Born to the bloodline, but the mark never manifested**

You carry dragonmarked blood but lack the mark itself. This grants you certain privileges while creating expectations you cannot fulfill.

**Ability Score Increase:** Choose any two different ability scores, each increases by +1  
**Feat:** Choose any feat for which you meet the prerequisites  
**Skill Proficiencies:** Choose two from any House-related skills  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Choose two appropriate to your House heritage  
**Equipment:** Fine clothes, house signet ring (indicating non-marked status), family heirloom, and a pouch containing 20 gp

**Feature - House Privilege:** Despite lacking a dragonmark, your bloodline grants access to House facilities and contacts, though with less influence than marked members.

### Aberrant Marked

**Bearer of a twisted, aberrant dragonmark**

Your dragonmark is wrong—twisted, painful, and marking you as an outcast from proper society. You've learned to hide your nature or embrace it.

**Ability Score Increase:** Constitution +2, Choose one other ability score +1  
**Feat:** Aberrant Dragonmark  
**Skill Proficiencies:** Deception, Survival  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Disguise kit, Herbalism kit  
**Equipment:** Disguise kit, dark cloak with hood, a journal detailing your mark's abilities, and a pouch containing 10 gp

**Feature - Underground Contacts:** Other aberrant marked recognize your plight. You can find shelter and information through hidden networks of outcasts and rebels.

## Using These Backgrounds

**Choose Appropriate Race:** Only specific races can manifest dragonmarks and take House backgrounds. Non-marked backgrounds are available to any race.

**Mark Manifestation:** Work with your DM to determine if your character actually bears a dragonmark or simply has House training. These backgrounds assume you have the mark unless you choose a non-marked variant.

**House Politics:** Your background connects you to one of Khorvaire's most powerful organizations, with all the benefits and obligations that entails.

**Regional Variations:** House culture varies by region. A Cannith artisan from Sharn differs from one raised in Aundair's magical academies.

**Advancement Options:** Characters with base dragonmarks can later take Greater Dragonmark feats as they advance in level, representing the growth and strengthening of their mark's power.

_For detailed information about dragonmark abilities and House operations, see the **Dragonmark Feats** section._
